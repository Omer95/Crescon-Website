About Us

Crescent Construction Company has a tradition of excellence, hard work and a can-do attitude. What began as a small construction outfit in the year 1995 has grown into a leading general contracting organization in Pakistan. Today our annual volume approaches over 1 billion rupees with fast growth rate. Quality, client satisfaction, business efficiency and safety are the four cornerstones of excellence that have propelled Crescent Construction Company forward as a trusted name in the Government, and Private sectors for timely completion of fast track projects. We aspire to be the builder of choice for construction of buildings, roads, pipelines and other infrastructure development projects. We also provide project management services and claims management for large projects. Our longevity and our future will always be tied into operating effectively and efficiently, and providing top notch services to our clients. We believe in setting extraordinary yet achievable goals and aim to conquer them. Roots of Crescent Construction Company have been firmly planted with belief in being fiscally responsible, service excellence and in supporting every employee to be able to achieve those results. Our professional staff manages construction process smoothly from the initial contact right through to completion.


Our Vision

To be a leading Services Group capable of undertaking construction business initiatives for local and international Clientele with commitment to succeed; promoting team work and individual creativity

Our Mission

Understand needs of the Clients and provide exceptional services to ensure their satisfaction by performing efficiently with uncompromised quality assurance and secure profitability for the company as well as for its Clients.
